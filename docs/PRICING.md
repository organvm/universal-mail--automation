# Pricing

**One categorization engine. Every inbox. Priced by how many you connect.**

Universal Mail Automation collapses the fragmented filtering systems of Gmail,
Outlook, iCloud, and IMAP into a single set of rules, one Eisenhower priority
model, and time-based escalation so nothing critical falls through the cracks.
Pricing scales with the only dimension that matters for that value: how many
mailboxes you unify behind one brain.

---

## Why it's worth paying for

Every email provider ships its own filter language. Gmail filters can't share
logic with Outlook rules; iCloud rules can't reference Gmail labels. The moment
you operate across more than one account, you're maintaining several
half-overlapping systems that do the same job badly — and you carry the constant
low-grade anxiety of missing the one message that mattered.

Universal Mail Automation replaces all of that with:

- **One taxonomy, every provider** — 28 hierarchical categories defined once,
  applied uniformly across Gmail, Outlook, iCloud, and any IMAP server.
- **Eisenhower prioritization** — every message lands in Critical / Important /
  Delegate / Reference, which decides what stays in the inbox, gets starred,
  gets archived, or is filed for retrieval.
- **Time-based escalation** — unaddressed, time-sensitive mail ages *up* in
  priority automatically. A reference email left for three days escalates to
  Critical instead of disappearing.
- **VIP routing** — named senders bypass normal rules and are forced to the top.
- **Voice-matched draft replies** — triage can draft responses in your own
  writing style for the items that actually need an answer.

The result: less time sorting, fewer dropped threads, one mental model instead
of three.

### Free — one inbox, fully triaged

For anyone who wants the engine on a single account. Connect one provider
(Gmail, Outlook, iCloud, or IMAP) and get the complete categorization, priority,
escalation, and VIP system at no cost. Ideal for trying the product or running a
single personal mailbox.

### Pro — $9/month, the multi-account workflow

For operators living across a personal Gmail, a work Outlook, and an iCloud
account. Connect **three or more providers** behind one set of rules, unlock
voice-matched draft replies, custom rules and taxonomy, and scheduled or
on-demand automation. This is where the "one brain for every inbox" value prop
pays for itself.

### Enterprise — custom, for teams

For teams and organizations needing unlimited connected mailboxes, multi-seat
provisioning, SSO, managed rule sets, onboarding, and a priority support SLA.
Pricing is tailored to seat count and integration needs.
